System requirements

 

The following are the minimum requirements your computer should comply with in order for you ro be able to enjoy full access to the content:

  1. Personal computer (Intel PC or Mac) with at least:
    • Pentium IV 1.5GHz Processor or better
    • 512 MB of RAM (1GB recommended)
    • 1024x768 pixel monitor
    • Speakers
    • Windows XP (or newer) or MAC OS X 10.3 (or newer)
    • Internet Explorer, Firefox, Mozilla or Safari browser
    • Adobe Flash player plugin for your browser version 10 or later
  2. Internet connection with at least 1 Mbps of Download speed/rate.
